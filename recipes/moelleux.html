Moelleux au Chocolat

The decadence of this moelleux au chocolat. also known as molten chocolate cake, lies in its simplicity.

Not only is this recipe easy to make, it is also extremely versatile. You can play around with different types of chocolate (bitter black chocolates to pistachio white ones) as well as add some fruits in the batter before placing it in the oven.

Ingredients

Butter
Both salted and non-salted butter can be used. I would recommend using non-salted butter but still adding a pinch of salt to the batter to add another dimension to the taste.
Sugar
This recipe calls for powdered sugar, but you can as easily use white sugar. Just beat beat the sugar and eggs until the mixture becomes light yellow to white-ish.

Ingredient list

  • 150g butter
  • 160g powdered sugar
  • 75g plain sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2oog eggs
  • 170g black chocolate

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190c
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter in a heat-proof bowl placed over a sauce pan with a small amount of simmering water
  3. In a seperate bowl, beat the eggs and sugar for 5 mins by hand or with a hand mixer until mixture turns light
  4. Fold the melted chocolate and butter mixture, as well as the flour, into the eggs and sugar mixture
  5. Pour the batter into the buttered ramekins and bake for about 7 mins
  6. Best enjoyed with ice cream, berry sauce, whipped cream, etc
