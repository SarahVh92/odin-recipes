Waffles

This easy American-style waffle mix will quickly become one of your go-to breakfasts or snacks.

It is not only easy to make, but it can also double as a great pancake mix if you don't have a waffle iron on hand.

Ingredients

Butter
Both salted and non-salted butter can be used. In case you use non-salted butter, add half a teaspoon of salt to the flour.
Sugar
As brown sugar is naturally moist, using it will result in a softer and moister waffles. White sugar, on the othe hand, will result in a slightly lighter-coloured and drier waffles.
I tend to just use whatever I have in my pantry though, as the flavour differences are negligible, but prefer brown sugar or a mix of brown and white.

Ingredient list

  • 250g plain flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 355ml warm milk
  • 75g butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix the other ingredients
  4. Fold the wet mix into the dry mix
  5. Ladle the batter into the preheated waffle iron. Cook until golden and crisp
  6. Best enjoyed with maple syrup or ice cream and fruits
